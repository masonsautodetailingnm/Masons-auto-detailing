Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?▾
You can book through our Booking page, or reach out by phone/text at 572-235-2991. We'll confirm within 24 hours.
Do you come to my location?▾
Yes! We come to your home, workplace, or wherever your vehicle is parked. We bring all our own supplies and equipment. We will need access to your water and a power outlet at the service location — just let us know where they are when we arrive.
Do I need to remove belongings before the appointment?▾
Yes. Please remove personal belongings from any areas you'd like cleaned. If personal items are left in a specific area, we will not clean that area. Clearing out ahead of time helps us do our best work.
How long does a detail take?▾
A basic exterior detail takes 1.5–2.5 hours. An interior takes 2–3 hours. A full detail can take 4–6+ hours for larger vehicles. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept cash, Zelle, Venmo, CashApp, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is due upon completion. No deposit required to book.
What if it rains on my appointment day?▾
If rain prevents exterior work, we'll reach out to reschedule at no charge. Interior work can typically proceed regardless of weather.
How does the referral program work?▾
It's simple — refer a friend to Mason's Mobile Detailing. When they book their first service, they get 20% off their total, and you receive $20 cash. Just have them mention your name when booking. There's no limit on how many friends you can refer!
What's your cancellation policy?▾
All canceled appointments are subject to a $25 cancellation fee per vehicle. Appointments may be rescheduled with at least 2 hours' notice. No-shows may also be subject to the $25 fee.
Is there a satisfaction guarantee?▾
Yes. If you're not satisfied, please let us know before we leave and we'll address it. We take pride in our work and won't consider a job done until you're happy.
