Maintenance Services

Already invested in a ceramic coating? Keep it performing at its best with a dedicated maintenance wash — specially formulated to clean without stripping your protection.

🧼
Ceramic Coating Maintenance Wash
Starting at $50 / all vehicle sizes
⏱️ ~45 minutes  ·  🛡️ Ceramic clients only
A specially formulated wash designed exclusively for ceramic-coated vehicles. Standard car washes and soaps can strip or degrade your coating over time — this wash cleans thoroughly without compromising the hydrophobic layer, keeping your protection performing like day one.
  • pH-neutral ceramic-safe wash solution
  • Hand wash & rinse
  • Wheel & tire cleaning
  • Windows cleaned (exterior)
  • Hydrophobic layer inspection
  • Available only to ceramic coating clients
💡 Why it matters

Ceramic coatings are an investment — regular maintenance washes extend the life of your coating and keep that deep, glossy, water-beading look going strong between full details. We recommend every 4–6 weeks.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do you need access to water and power?▾
    Yes — we bring all our own equipment and supplies, but we do require access to a water source (outdoor spigot works great) and a standard power outlet at your location. If you're unsure whether your setup will work, just mention it in your quote request or give us a call and we'll sort it out.
    What payment methods do you accept?▾
    We accept Cash, Zelle, Venmo, CashApp, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is due upon completion of the service. No deposit is required to book.
    How far in advance should I book?▾
    We recommend booking at least 2–3 days in advance to secure your preferred date and time, especially on weekends. Same-week appointments may be available depending on the schedule — reach out by phone or text at 572-235-2991 for the fastest response on short-notice requests.
    How do I book an appointment?▾
    Start by getting a free quote through the Get a Free Quote form or by calling/texting 572-235-2991. Once we've confirmed the details and price, use the Book Now button to lock in your date and time — no deposit required.
    Do you come to my location?▾
    Yes! We come to your home, workplace, or wherever your vehicle is parked. We bring all our own supplies and equipment. We will need access to your water and a power outlet at the service location — just let us know where they are when we arrive.
    Do I need to remove belongings before the detail?▾
    Yes. Please remove personal belongings from any areas you'd like cleaned. If personal items are left in a specific area, we will not clean that area. Clearing out ahead of time helps us do our best work.
    How long does a detail take?▾
    A basic exterior detail takes 1.5–2.5 hours. An interior takes 2–3 hours. A full detail can take 4–6+ hours for larger vehicles. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.
    What payment methods do you accept?▾
    We accept cash, Zelle, Venmo, CashApp, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is due upon completion. No deposit required to book.
    What if it rains on my appointment day?▾
    If rain prevents exterior work, we'll reach out to reschedule at no charge. Interior work can typically proceed regardless of weather.
    How does the referral program work?▾
    It's simple — refer a friend to Mason's Mobile Detailing. When they book their first service, they get 20% off their total, and you receive $20 cash. Just have them mention your name when booking. There's no limit on how many friends you can refer!
    What's your cancellation policy?▾
    All canceled appointments are subject to a $25 cancellation fee per vehicle. Appointments may be rescheduled with at least 2 hours' notice. No-shows may also be subject to the $25 fee.
    Is there a satisfaction guarantee?▾
    Yes. If you're not satisfied, please let us know before we leave and we'll address it. We take pride in our work and won't consider a job done until you're happy.
